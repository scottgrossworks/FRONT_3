Badges
The badge system is a way to reward users for their activity on the Leedz and give buyers confidence in the leedz they see posted. Users earn badges as they reach milestones for leedz posted, bought and sold. When you see a seller who has earned Leedz accolades, you know they are a trusted user of the system.
